Question: When are you planting?

Rationale statement:

With fertilisation at planting the timing of planting affects the timing relative to the wet season and its associated risk of higher N loss. For an early planted and fertilised crop, the NI is unlikely to still work by the time this risk of N loss increases, hence a slow releasing CRF is likely to give more benefit.
